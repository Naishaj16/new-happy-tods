Our Programs

Tailored learning experiences for every stage of childhood.

Programs designed for meaningful early learning.

At Happy Tods Chennai, our programs are age-appropriate, play-based, and thoughtfully paced to respect the unique journey of every child. We focus on intellectual curiosity, emotional security, and social growth.

Our Programs

1.5 - 2.5 YEARS

Toddler Program

Focusing on sensory exploration, language development, and the first steps toward social interaction in a gentle, warm setting.

2.5 - 3.5 YEARS

Preschool Program

Encouraging active curiosity, symbolic play, and emotional resilience through a rich environment of arts, nature, and community.

3.5 - 4.5 YEARS

Pre-Kindergarten

Developing complex social bonds, early literacy, and problem-solving skills through collaborative projects and inquiry-led play.

4.5 - 5.5 YEARS

Kindergarten

Preparing children for the future with confidence, literacy foundations, and a deep, lasting love for learning and discovery.

Nurturing Progression

As children grow, their learning needs evolve. We provide a balance between structure and freedom, ensuring a safe, nurturing progression where every milestone is celebrated with respect and care.

Balance & Freedom

We provide a thoughtfully designed environment that encourages independent choice while offering the security of a calm, predictable daily rhythm.

Our Pedagogical Pillars

Rooted in respect, curiosity, and emotional safety. We believe that when children feel seen and safe, their natural desire to learn flourishes effortlessly.
